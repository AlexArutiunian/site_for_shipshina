    • Raw unsalted cow milk cheese

  • Young cow's milk cheese is a completely raw product without added salt, enzymes and without heating. Ingredients: 100% milk, which retains a maximum of useful substances and turns into cheese with the help of bacteria that independently develop in the product itself.

    • Nomad cheese

  • For the production of Motal cheese, curd prepared by natural souring, is densely stuffed into a peeled goat skin sack through the neck. The opening of the case is closed with a woolen ribbon. Then, according to the ancient tradition of the nomads of Iran, the goat skin with cheese will be buried in the ground for 6 months or for a year.
